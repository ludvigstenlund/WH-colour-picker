General table for paint usage:

Type: Use: Example:
Base: Used to get the main colour onto the miniature. Usually the first step with a brush.
Image by Warhammer on YouTube
Layer: Used to tint and change the undercolour. Way thinner when compared to base paints.
Image by Warhammer on YouTube
Shade: Used to tint the colour underneath and pool in recesses. This darkens these areas and makes for artificial shadows
Image by Kom.se
Keep in mind: These uses are only recommendations. Use your paint however you feel like.
